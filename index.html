A B O U T _ M E

Hi again! My name is Roberta D'Agostino, I'm 20 years old and I'm studying to become a Junior Front-end Developer.
I started this new adventure to free my creativity in an active way, enhancing my visual communication skills and improving my ability to work in a team. I do believe in the power of the heterogeneity while teamworking, because everyone's uniques carateristiques can be an incredible source of different points of wiew, different approaches to problems and the possibilities to do a really good job are surely increased.
By the way, I bet you can imagine how extroverted I am (despite the fact I look a lil shy at first, but I'm working on it). I do love exchanging ideas and doing inspiring conversation with everyone can give me an opportunity to learn sometihing. I know that you may be interested on knowing things like my strenghts and my weaknesses, so... Here they are! My best feature is that I am so curious: I love asking questions, observing details and creating folders in my brain related to everything (sometimes it's a weakness because informations overlap each others and I start remembering things like the song I sang in kindergarden to my mom forgetting important things like what I've just red or what I ate for lunch)
Now it's time to let you know what my bigger weakness is: my sensibility. I know it may seam a good thing but...trust me, when you start feel bad just because someone you care about is facing something that makes him suffer it becomes so invalidating (but I'm working on it, I swear.)
And that's it, I don't want to be boring writing a too-long paraghaph, if you want to get to know me better I would be pleased to talk with you!
